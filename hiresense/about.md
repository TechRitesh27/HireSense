The Interview Intelligence Engine is the core evaluation module of HireSense. 
It extends the existing candidate lifecycle — which covers 
    candidate import, 
    status tracking, 
    hiring drive management 
with a full end-to-end interview pipeline. 
The pipeline spans five concerns: 
    assigning interviewers to candidates, 
    extracting structured intelligence from candidate resumes, 
    auto-generating AI-powered interview questions, 
    executing interviews with structured answer evaluation, 
    and ranking candidates after interview completion.

The three roles in scope are `COMPANY_ADMIN`, `INTERVIEWER`, and `SUPER_ADMIN`. All new modules must follow SOLID principles and be extensible via Strategy/Factory/Observer patterns to accommodate future modules such as HR policy checks and offer management.
